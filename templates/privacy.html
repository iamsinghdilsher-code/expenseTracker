Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense data you enter — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information.

2. How We Use It

Your data is used solely to power your Spendly account — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and improve your experience. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party analytics that collect personal data.

5. Third Parties

We use Google Fonts to load typefaces for the interface. This request is made directly from your browser to Google's servers. Apart from this, we do not integrate third-party services that receive your personal data.

6. Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings. You may also request a copy of your data by contacting us directly.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at hello@spendly.app.
